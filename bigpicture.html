Big Picture Challenge

Big Picture Challenge has users identify zoomed-in photos to solve over 300 puzzles, encouraging parents to play the game alongside their kids and learn facts about the photos together.

Made available on on all iOS, Android, and Kindle devices, Big Picture Challenge was PBS' first general audience (GA) gaming app in the market, and thus had to establish a visual style that was 1) appropriate for all ages and 2) paved the way for future PBS GA gaming apps to come.

Role: Visual Design, Branding/Identity, Logo, Promo Graphics | Developed by Juxta Labs
